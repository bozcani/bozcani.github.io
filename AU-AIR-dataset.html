# AU-AIR Dataset AU-AIR is a multi-modal aerial dataset captured by a UAV. Having visual data, object annotations, and flight data (time, GPS, altitude, IMU sensor data, velocities), AU-AIR meets vision and robotics for UAVs. ## Specifications - 8 raw RGB videos (more than 2 hours in total) - 32,283 extracted and labelled frames - Bounding box annotations for eight objects related to traffic: - human, car, van, truck, bike, motorbike, bus, trailar - Time, GPS, altitude, IMU sensor data and linear velocities of the drone are avaliable for each extracted frame.
